About Us: Experienced and Creative Garden Maintenance

For over a decade, I’ve been providing professional grounds and estate maintenance across Heathfield and the surrounding areas. Dedicated, high-quality grass cutting services and lawn care form the true foundation of my business. From regular residential mowing to large-scale ride-on paddock management, keeping your lawn pristine is what I do best. I also offer additional services to complete your outdoor space, including precision hedge trimming, comprehensive garden clearance, and premium hardwood log delivery.


I pride myself on building strong relationships with my clients, and the majority of my work comes directly from their recommendations. Whether I'm managing a private residential garden or fulfilling a commercial contract for a local pub or business, I work closely with you to meet your exact requirements. I always leave every site spotlessly clean and tidy, so your outdoor space is ready to be enjoyed the moment I leave. Additionally, I provide expert tree work and fencing to enhance the beauty and security of your property.
